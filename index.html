Where A Traditional Past Meets A Modern Future.

Travel

Once a mudbrick waystation along desert trading routes, Riyadh is now one of the wealthiest cities in the world. The country's political, financial, and administrative capital is also a city with fascinating subtext.

History

Riyadh (/rɪˈjɑːd/; Arabic: الرياض ar-Riyāḍ pronounced [ar.riˈjaːdˤ], "The Gardens") is the capital and largest city of Saudi Arabia.
